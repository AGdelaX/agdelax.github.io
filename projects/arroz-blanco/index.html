Arroz Blanco

Last year, out of curiosity I decided to check out the book Launch a Kickass T-Shirt Brand. Inspired by other hispanic T-shirt brands like Tripleta and MangoBajito, I decided to give it a shot and that summer I set up a booth in Puerto Rico Comic Con selling t-shirts and stickers I designed. The product line consisted of 6 t-shirts and 3 stickers.

The designs were inspired by a mix of Puerto Rican and Japanese culture, hence the name Arroz Blanco—since both Japanese and Puerto Rican cuisine have a heavy emphasis on white rice. I also set up a basic website and social media campaign promoting the products.

The booth was successful, with over 150 shirts sold and lots of buzz from locals.
