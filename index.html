Hello! I'm a designer, web developer and illustrator from New York. I'm currently the Lead Product Designer at Stayawhile, in charge of their UI/UX and front-end development. Before that I've worked on design at places like ArtBinder and Alice, and have also launched an app of my own. I believe design is visual engineering. When I work I always think about the content before the form. I am skilled in UI/UX design, graphic design, illustration and front-end development. Outside of work I like webcomics, video games and good beer.
Hit me up if you ever need a designer.
