Hello! I'm a designer and web developer from Puerto Rico now living in NYC. I've worked on design at places like ArtBinder and Alice, and have recently launched an app of my own. I am skilled in UI/UX design, graphic design, illustration and front-end development. Outside of work I like comics, video games and good beer. Hit me up if you ever need a designer.

Projects

Arithmatic—An educational mobile game for iPhone, players must solve arithmetic questions as quickly as possible and compete in leaderboards.

ArtBinder Personas—Persona development and user research for the large range of ArtBinder's customers and products.

Alice Concierge CRM—A CRM for hotel concierge as a part of Alice's backend hotel management solution.

divPixels—A free to use, browser-based pixel art tool for quickly developing pixel drawings.

White with Red—A series of illustrations and web animations based on the short horror story of the same name.

Arroz Blanco—A t-shirt and sticker line I developed and sold in Puerto Rico Comic Con. The brand is a blend of otaku and Puerto Rican culture.
