I am a designer and developer from Puerto Rico now studying and working in NYC. Since I am versed in both the creative and technical sides of developing an application, I want to be closely involved with the user experience and implementation of client side interfaces. I am also interested in data visualization and interactive art.

I am currently working on my BFA in Design from the School of Visual Arts and have recently completed a Front End Development Apprenticeship with Bloc.

Outside of work I am an animation, comics and film buff. I used to study Illustration, but I also strongly considered going to school for Art History. I enjoy drinking craft beer and sake with friends and trying new types of cuisine. I love single player video games, although I haven’t been able to sit down and play in quite a long time. You can probably find me spinning in Flywheel's Flatiron location.

Projects

Arroz Blanco (T-Shirt and Sticker Design, Branding)

As a summer project, I sought to launch a t-shirt and sticker business and sell the merchandise in Puerto Rico Comic Con. Although the business wasn’t a success, this project is what got me interested in UI/UX Design.

Koloni (UI/UX Design, Branding)

As a semester-long assignment I designed the screens, branding and marketing of a fictional microfinance app I named Koloni.

Arithmatic (UI/UX Design)

Designed for my App Design class, Arithmatic is a fast paced, mobile game in which players solve arithmetic problems as quickly as possible. I’m interested in developing this concept further.

BlocChat (Front End Development)

My first project in Bloc without training wheels, I built a real time chat room with Angular running on the client side and Firebase as persistent data.

Header/Body Font ‘Randomizer’ (Front End Development)

A quick little project, I used Vanilla JS to write a script that lets users add Google Fonts to two sets of arrays of objects, one for header fonts and the other for body fonts. The script will ‘randomize’ the fonts displayed on the page.
